Privacy Policy for Royal Court: Card Scorekeeper Last updated: 2026-03-22 Royal Court: Card Scorekeeper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Photos: Game photos are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to any server, but can be shared only via local sync or backups.. Location: GPS coordinates are captured only when you explicitly start a game, stored locally on your device, and used solely to tag where games were played. Location data is never transmitted externally, but can be shared only via local sync or backups. WiFi Sync: Device-to-device sync operates only on your local WiFi network using PIN-secured encryption. No data passes through any external server. Backups: Export files are created locally and shared only through your device's share sheet. We have no access to your backups. This app contains no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that collect data. Contact: apps@wehrenterprises.org
